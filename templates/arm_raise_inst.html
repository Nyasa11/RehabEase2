Arm Raise

Instructions

Stand tall with your feet shoulder-width apart.

Lift your arms forward or to the side, keeping your palms facing down.

Raise your arms until they are parallel to the ground.

Slowly lower them back to the starting position.

Maintain a controlled, smooth movement throughout the exercise.

Reference Video

Safety Notes

Start with light weights or no weights for beginners.

Focus on smooth, controlled motions.

Do not overextend your shoulders.

Stop if you experience any shoulder pain.

Exercise Metrics

Repetitions: 3 sets of 10-15 reps.

Rest time: 30-45 seconds between sets.

Recommended speed: Slow and controlled.
