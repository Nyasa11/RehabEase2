Single Leg Stand Front

Instructions

Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart.

Shift your weight to one leg and lift the other leg facing the front.

Hold this position for a few seconds, then lower the leg.

Repeat with the other leg.

Keep your body upright and your core engaged.

Reference Video

Safety Notes

Do not lean while balancing.

If you feel unstable, use a chair or wall for support.

Stop if you feel any sharp pain in your knee or ankle.

Perform the movement slowly and in a controlled manner.

Exercise Metrics

Repetitions: 3 sets of 10 reps per leg.

Hold time: 5-10 seconds per rep.

Rest time: 30 seconds between sets.

Recommended speed: Slow and controlled.
