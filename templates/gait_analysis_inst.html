Gait Analysis

Instructions

Stand up straight with your feet hip-width apart.

Begin by walking forward at a comfortable pace.

Ensure that you maintain a neutral posture, keeping your head up and shoulders relaxed.

Pay attention to your stride length and foot placement as you walk.

Focus on keeping your movements smooth and controlled throughout the analysis.

Reference Video

Safety Notes

Wear comfortable footwear that provides proper support.

If you experience any pain or discomfort while walking, stop the analysis immediately.

Ensure you are walking in a safe environment with ample space to move freely.

Analysis Metrics

Stride length: Aim for an even and consistent stride length.

Foot placement: Ensure that your feet land directly beneath your hips for optimal alignment.

Posture: Keep your back straight and shoulders relaxed throughout the analysis.
