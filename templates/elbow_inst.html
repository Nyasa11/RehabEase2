Elbow Flexion & Extension

Instructions

Rest your forearm on a table with your wrist over the edge.

Palm should face down.

Slowly lift your hand upward.

Return to the starting position.

Keep movements smooth and controlled.

Reference Video

Safety Notes

Use a comfortable range of motion.

Keep your forearm stable.

Stop if you feel wrist pain.

Avoid quick or jerky movements.

Exercise Metrics

Repetitions: 3 sets of 10 reps.

Rest time: 30 seconds between sets.

Recommended speed: Slow and controlled.
