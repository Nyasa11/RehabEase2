Knee Flexion & Extension

Instructions

Sit with your legs extended and feet flat on the floor.

Slowly bend your knee towards your chest.

Extend your leg back to the starting position.

Keep movements slow and controlled throughout.

Reference Video

Safety Notes

Do not overextend your knee.

Perform the movement slowly to avoid injury.

Stop if you feel sharp knee pain.

Avoid quick or jerky movements.

Exercise Metrics

Repetitions: 3 sets of 10 reps.

Rest time: 30 seconds between sets.

Recommended speed: Slow and controlled.
